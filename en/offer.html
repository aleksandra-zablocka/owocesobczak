Offer

We invite you to familiarize yourself with our offer:

  • 1. Red Pinova

    Large, spherical-conical fruit, covered with an orange-red, fuzzy blush of light yellow, medium juicy, sweet and sour tasty flesh.
    Calibres from 65+ to 95+.

  • 2. Jonaprince

    Large fruit, with a dark red blush. Creamy skin and red-orange blush. Fragile and extremely juicy with a sweet and sour taste.
    Calibres from 65+ to 95+.

  • 3. Gala Selekt

    Fruits are medium, spherical or spherical-conical. The skin is smooth greenish-yellow with intense, dark and fuzzy color blush. The flesh is creamy, fine-grained, juicy, crisp, sweet and very tasty.
    Calibres from 65+ to 95+.

  • 4. Gala Schniga

    Small or medium-sized fruits with a spherical-conical shape. The skin is smooth, golden yellow, covered dark red blush. The flesh is creamy, sweet, crisp, juicy and tasty.
    Calibres from 65+ to 95+.

In addition to selling fruit, we also offer storage, sorting, packaging and packaging services transport of fruit and vegetables:

  • 1. Storage

    We offer fruit and vegetable storage services in high-class storage chambers, including: controlled atmosphere (temperature, relative humidity, composition of the storage atmosphere).

  • 2. Sorting

    We can sort selected fruits and vegetables in terms of size, weight, color and quality, using water unloading, hydrovacuum filling machine and a system of 26 water channels, thanks which allows apples to be divided into selected categories.

  • 3. Packing

    Available packaging: plastic bags, cardboard packaging (bushel), wooden boxes, crates plastic, box pallets.

  • 4. Transport

    We offer transport of fruit and vegetables using refrigerated vehicles that can maintain a constant temperature within the indicated scope.
