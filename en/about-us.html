About us

We have been cultivating our passion for growing and selling apples for generations. Every year we carefully maintain our orchards (including: preparing sites, selecting varieties, nursery material, cutting, thinning or fertilizing), so that the harvested apples meet the highest standards of quality and environmental care. It sets us apart individual approach to the client, flexibility and building partnership relationships and willingness to establish long-term cooperation. We are constantly trying to develop our company, based on family, timeless values ​​and using the latest, innovative technological solutions. We have GLOBAL GAP and HACAP certificates. We follow the principles of Integrated Apple Production (IP). Trust customers are most important to us, which is why we try to establish lasting, direct relationships by being in open to dialogue at all times.

We deliver the highest quality products and services and provide excellent customer service, both on stage of preparation of the offer, based on the needs and requirements of customers, through effective implementation orders with an optimally prepared product, delivered at the indicated place and time, up to service after-sales, including technical support, assistance in possible complaints, and collection opinions and suggestions from customers. Our apples are available all year round, maintaining the highest quality quality, rational prices and efficient logistics. We offer several varieties of apples, but upon request We are also able to deliver other fruits and vegetables to the customer.

We invite you to join us on our journey!
