What we do

Our offer includes the highest quality, natural and healthy apples grown, harvested, stored, calibrated, packed and delivered with the utmost care. Our offer is directed for both wholesale and retail customers. In addition to apples, we also sell other fruits, vegetables and offer storage facilities and logistics services.

As a multigenerational company, we combine many years of family tradition and passion for fruit growing, with a modern and professional approach to customers, each time tailoring the offer to individual needs and requirements. We are constantly developing, trying to meet the expectations of the most demanding customers.
